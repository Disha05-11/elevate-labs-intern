# Phishing Email Analysis Report

## Objective
Analyze a suspicious email and identify phishing indicators.

## Email Details

Sender: support-paypal-security@gmail.com

Subject: URGENT! Your Account Will Be Suspended

## Phishing Indicators Found

### 1. Suspicious Sender Address
The sender uses a Gmail address instead of an official PayPal domain.

### 2. Urgent Language
The email pressures the recipient to act immediately.

### 3. Suspicious URL
http://paypal-security-verification.free-login.com

This domain is not owned by PayPal.

### 4. Threat-Based Messaging
The email threatens account suspension within 24 hours.

### 5. Generic Greeting
Uses "Dear Customer" instead of the recipient's real name.

### 6. Email Spoofing Attempt
The sender impersonates PayPal to gain trust.

## Risks

- Credential theft
- Financial fraud
- Identity theft

## Conclusion

The email contains multiple phishing characteristics and should be treated as malicious.
